WHAT IS STREAMING?
○ Streaming refers to watching videos or listening to audio content without having the need to download it first.

WHAT ARE THE TYPES AND PLATFORMS OF STREAMING?
○ You may watch or listen to many different types of streaming such as movies, music videos, video games, TV shows,
and others on streaming platforms like Netflix, Spotify, YouTube, Twitch, Facebook, etc. Note that it is important that you
are able to upload appropriate content to your designated streaming platform.

NEED OF VIEWERS
Online Gaming may appear to be good for building up character development, but the lack of self-limitation of
teenage students to this is closely linked to online gaming addiction, greatly affecting our well-being, whether socially,
mentally, physically, or emotionally. Thus, the viewers need to:
○ Know how online gaming addiction affects the overall health of a player.
○ Manage their time when playing online games to avoid addiction.
○ Analyze what online games are appropriate for their age.
○ Learn ways how to overcome online gaming addiction.

CHARACTERISTICS OF A GOOD STREAM

1. Know the trends.
Know how online gaming addiction affects the overall health of a player.
When starting a stream or uploading content, it's important to
keep up with the trends.This makes your content more interesting
and engaging and it helps you create content that your
audience wants as well, allowing you to increase the number
of your viewers.

2. Be clear.
Your content should be clear to make your audience’s
viewing experience understandable.

3. Engage.
Engaging with your audience keeps things enjoyable and gets
people to stick around. Low chance of boredom and
a high chance of fun!

4. Be energetic.
It's best to maintain a fun stream with energetic energy as this is one
of the most important keys to having a successful stream.

5. Don't forget to be professional!
Executing professionalism in appearance, way of talking, gestures, and the overall process of streaming allow your
content to be appropriate for a variety of individuals.

Frequently Asked Questions
1. What is Live Streaming?
Live Streaming is type of streaming that refers to a media content that allows us to watch and/or listen in real time.
During live streaming, pausing the video or audio content may not be done. You cannot go back to where you left it.

2. What are some common public live streaming applications?
Some of the public live streaming applications include Facebook Live, Twitter, Instagram Live Stories, V Live, Twitch TV,
Tik Tok, and YouTube Live.
